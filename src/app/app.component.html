Portfolio : A collection of resources where the creators showcase to get recognizedCreating an online portfolio can help you establish a professional online presence and showcase your work in a polished way¹³. It can also become a source of inspiration for other creative-minded people². Here are some benefits of creating an online portfolio: 1. Professional way to showcase your work: Building a website about your brand and experience is a polished way to share your expertise with others¹. 2. Great first impression for employers: An online portfolio provides a visual and verbal example of the level of dedication you put into your craft². 3. Increases your visibility and online presence: When an employer Googles your name, your professional portfolio will be one of the first search results that pops up³. 4. Shows you’re more than just a resume: Because of the flexibility of an online portfolio, you’re able to show your personality by choosing design, layout and the copy you use³. 5. Flexibility: Unlike other platforms, a website allows you to create a specific image and establish your brand². I hope this helps!
